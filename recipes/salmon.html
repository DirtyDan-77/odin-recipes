Garlic Butter Herb Salmon

Description

This Garlic Butter Herb Salmon with Roasted Vegetables is a simple yet elegant dinner that’s perfect for a cozy night in. Fresh salmon fillets are topped with a rich garlic herb butter infused with lemon, parsley, and thyme, then roasted alongside a colorful medley of zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, and cherry tomatoes. Everything cooks on a single sheet pan in under 20 minutes, making cleanup a breeze. The result is a flavorful, well-balanced meal that feels special without requiring much effort—ideal for impressing guests or treating someone you love.

Ingredients

For the salmon:

    2–3 salmon fillets (skin on or off) 3 tbsp butter 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tsp lemon zest 1 tbsp lemon juice 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tsp dried) 1 tsp chopped thyme (or ½ tsp dried) Salt & pepper to taste

For the vegetables:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prep the veggies: Toss the chopped veggies in olive oil, salt, pepper, and a sprinkle of dried oregano. Spread them out on one side of a baking sheet.
  3. Make garlic herb butter: In a small bowl, combine butter, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Prep the salmon: Pat the fillets dry. Place them on the other side of the baking sheet. Spread the garlic herb butter evenly on top.
  5. Roast everything for about 15–18 minutes, until the salmon is flaky and the veggies are tender. If you want a slightly crisp top, broil the salmon for an extra 2 minutes at the end.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges, maybe a side of crusty bread or a simple salad.
