Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

Description

This Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken is juicy, flavorful, and bursting with bright citrus flavor. A mix of garlic, rosemary, thyme, and lemon juice is rubbed under the skin for maximum taste. Roasted to golden perfection, it’s perfect for a hearty dinner or a meal prep staple.

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp chopped rosemary
  • 1 tsp chopped thyme
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Optional sides:

  • Roasted potatoes
  • Steamed green beans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub the mixture under the chicken skin and on top.
  5. Place chicken on a baking sheet or oven-safe skillet.
  6. Roast for 35–40 minutes until skin is golden and internal temp is 165°F (74°C).
  7. Let rest 5 minutes before serving. Serve with sides of your choice.
