Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta

Description

This Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta is a quick, comforting dish that brings together the richness of a cream sauce with the brightness of tomatoes and fresh basil. Perfect for weeknight dinners, this vegetarian recipe is sure to be a household favorite.

Ingredients

For the pasta:

  • 8 oz fettuccine or penne pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Optional toppings:

  • Extra Parmesan
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, stir, and let simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat and stir in heavy cream. Let simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Mix in Parmesan cheese and chopped basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Serve with extra Parmesan and red pepper flakes if desired.
